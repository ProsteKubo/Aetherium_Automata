\chapter{Contents of the Included Storage Media}
\label{app:media}

% TODO: fill in final directory listing once all deliverables are collected.

The included storage medium contains the following items:

\begin{description}
  \item[\texttt{src/}] Full source code of the Aetherium Automata platform:
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \texttt{src/engine/} --- C++17 runtime engine and platform targets
      \item \texttt{src/ide/} --- Electron IDE (TypeScript/React)
      \item \texttt{src/gateway/} --- Elixir/Phoenix gateway
      \item \texttt{src/server/} --- Elixir server
    \end{itemize}
  \item[\texttt{example/}] Showcase automata catalog:
    \begin{itemize}
      \item \texttt{example/automata/showcase/} --- 18 YAML automata definitions
      \item \texttt{example/ide\_demo\_projects/} --- IDE demo project files (\texttt{.aeth})
    \end{itemize}
  \item[\texttt{thesis/}] \LaTeX{} source of this technical report and compiled PDF.
\end{description}

The complete field reference for the YAML domain-specific language is given below.
Fields marked \textbf{(R)} are required; fields marked \textbf{(O)} are optional.

\begin{table}[H]
  \centering
  \caption{Top-level YAML fields.}
  \vskip6pt
  \begin{tabular}{lll}
    \toprule
    Field & Required & Description \\
    \midrule
    \texttt{version} & R & Schema version (currently \texttt{0.0.1}) \\
    \texttt{config} & R & Automaton metadata block \\
    \texttt{variables} & O & List of variable definitions \\
    \texttt{automata} & R & EFSM body \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
\end{table}

\begin{table}[H]
  \centering
  \caption{\texttt{config} block fields.}
  \vskip6pt
  \begin{tabular}{lll}
    \toprule
    Field & Required & Description \\
    \midrule
    \texttt{name} & R & Human-readable automaton name \\
    \texttt{type} & R & \texttt{inline} or \texttt{folder} \\
    \texttt{location} & R if \texttt{folder} & Path to folder containing code files \\
    \texttt{language} & O & Scripting language; default \texttt{lua} \\
    \texttt{description} & O & Free-text description \\
    \texttt{author} & O & Author name \\
    \texttt{tags} & O & List of string tags for discovery \\
    \bottomrule
  \end{tabular}
\end{table}

\begin{table}[H]
  \centering
  \caption{Transition type-specific fields.}
  \vskip6pt
  \begin{tabular}{lll}

    \texttt{classic}       & \texttt{condition}                    & \texttt{priority}, \texttt{weight} \\

    \texttt{event}         & \texttt{event.triggers}               & \texttt{event.require\_all}, \texttt{priority} \\
    \texttt{probabilistic} & \texttt{weight}                       & \texttt{priority} \\
    \texttt{immediate}     & ---                                   & \texttt{priority} \\

  \end{tabular}
\end{table}
